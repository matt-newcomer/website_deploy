Social Commentary

24 January 2023

dystopia (n.)

a very bad or unfair society in which there is a lot of suffering, especially an imaginary society in the future, after something terrible has happened; a description of such a society.

The following questions are best pondered alone and with a clear head:

Is society unfair? Why is society bad or unfair? How did it get this way?

Where is the suffering? Who experiences it? Why does it exist?

In the set of all possible futures, how many are worse than now? How did they get there?

What was the terrible event that began the chain reaction? How did the reaction run away uncontrolled?

What did/do/will I do to fuel or inhibit these changes? Am I an idle observer?

Did you experience your head effortlessly substituting in certain words and phrases? Maybe we didn’t use the same words, but I think you get the point.
