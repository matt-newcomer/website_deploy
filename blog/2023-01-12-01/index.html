Stormy Days

12 January 2023

Life changes quickly, yet somehow it moves even faster.

Packed schedules, to-do lists, and obligations keep the accelerator pinned to the floor.

Nuanced opinions, complicated feelings, and distant aspirations cloud your mind.

The world around just keeps moving.

Why not stop, even if only for a moment?

The rain rhythmically drips down the window and flows down the gutter.

The dark skies occlude the brighter days ahead.

Let the change of weather envelop you; be with yourself apart from the world that surrounds you.

The lightning reminds you of the rushes left to experiences, thunder shakes you at your core.

Wake up in the morning, pick up the fallen branches, and move on.
