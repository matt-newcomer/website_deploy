100 Days To Offload

7 December 2022

I’m not that great at writing.

Whenever I think about writing, I feel a big swell of anxiety and inevitably say I’ll do it tomorrow. It’s primarily because hitting post feels like yelling into the void for someone to read my thoughts. The Internet also has a magical way of letting words stick around forever.

Pretty scary stuff.

Recently, I found 100 Days To Offload. It’s a challenge to write and upload 100 posts within a year. The challenge sounds like a weird way to jumpstart your blog with this 1 easy trick they don’t want you to know about, but it’s more a deadline for me to work against.

From 1 January 2023 to 31 December 2023, I plan to upload 100 things.

Plain and simple.
