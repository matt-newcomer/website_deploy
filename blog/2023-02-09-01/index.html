ChatGPT Is a Solution in Search of a Problem

9 February 2023

Recently, large language models (LLMs) have been near the center of the collective consciousness. Numerous humorous and enlightening Twitter threads have been authored about what promise the future holds as the technology improves and the parameter count grows. Not to be a luddite, but I think that the trial-by-fire that we’ve witnessed over the past few months is definitive proof that we need to reevaluate our connection with the technology.
